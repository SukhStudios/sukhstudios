Intro

Welcome to Sukh Studios.
I'm Sukh, a creative designer and 3D visual artist passionate about crafting detailed, high-quality visuals that bring ideas and products to life. My work focuses on precision, realism, and clean design, ensuring that every project not only looks good but communicates purpose and value..

At Sukh Studios, I specialize in 3D product visualization, realistic renders, and modern design solutions tailored for brands, creators, and businesses. From concept development to final output, I pay close attention to lighting, materials, composition, and detail, because great design lives in the details.

I believe strong visuals have the power to elevate brands, tell stories, and create lasting impressions. Whether it's a product render, a visual concept, or a creative design, my goal is always the same: to deliver work that feels refined, impactful, and professionally crafted.

This portfolio is a reflection of my skills, process, and creative journey. Every project you see here represents my commitment to quality, consistency, and continuous improvement.
Let's create visuals that stand out and speak for themselves.

Work

rolex

A cinematic product animation showcasing the timeless elegance of a luxury aviation-inspired wristwatch.
Set against a deep black background, the polished stainless-steel case and bracelet catch subtle highlights, emphasizing precision craftsmanship and premium finishing.

The bold black dial features high-contrast white numerals for maximum readability, while the smooth sweep of the second hand reflects mechanical excellence and accuracy. Carefully controlled lighting reveals refined edges, brushed textures, and the iconic crown, creating a powerful sense of depth and realism.

This animation focuses on heritage, precision, and luxury, delivering a strong visual statement designed for high-end branding, advertising, and product showcases.

creed

A dramatic static render capturing the essence of timeless luxury and bold masculinity. The fragrance bottle stands emerging from deep shadows, surrounded by rugged stone textures that symbolize strength, heritage, and raw sophistication. Minimal lighting is used to sculpt the silhouette, revealing only the most essential details and creating a powerful sense of mystery.

The accompanying close-up highlights the finely crafted cap, where a warm golden emblem cuts through the darkness. This contrast between matte black surfaces and glowing gold accents emphasizes premium craftsmanship and exclusivity.

Designed with a cinematic approach, this render focuses on contrast, texture, and controlled illumination, making it ideal for high-end fragrance branding, advertising visuals, and luxury product showcases.

google pixel headphones

A conceptual product animation that explores the harmony between intelligent design and premium craftsmanship. The headphones float effortlessly above a minimal stone pedestal, symbolizing balance, precision, and advanced technology. Soft, directional lighting glides across the matte and metallic surfaces, revealing smooth curves, refined textures, and a carefully engineered form.

As the animation unfolds, subtle motion and controlled camera movement emphasize comfort, acoustic depth, and thoughtful ergonomics. The interplay of light and shadow highlights the contrast between warm metallic tones and deep black cushions, creating a sophisticated yet futuristic visual language.

Designed as an AI driven concept advertisement, this animation focuses on clarity, elegance, and innovation communicating a next-generation listening experience built around design intelligence, premium materials, and immersive sound.
